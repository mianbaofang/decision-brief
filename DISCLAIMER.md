## English

Decision Brief is a local-first decision-support application. It helps users structure uncertainty into evidence, risks, options, and next steps. It does not make decisions for users and does not provide legal, medical, mental-health, financial, investment, tax, insurance, immigration, education, employment, family-law, or other professional advice.

Do not rely on this project as the sole basis for high-stakes decisions. For serious matters, consult qualified professionals, trusted adults, teachers, doctors, lawyers, financial advisers, or relevant authorities.

The Fengshui and Nature lenses are for perspective, reflection, and entertainment. They must not be treated as factual predictions, medical advice, investment signals, or guaranteed decision rules.

User data and privacy:

- The app stores decision archives locally in SQLite by default.
- If the user configures an LLM provider, text and image inputs may be sent to that third-party provider.
- If the user uses TTS, text may be sent to the configured speech service.
- If weather features are enabled, city or location-related requests may be sent to the weather provider.
- Users should not upload private documents, identity cards, medical records, contracts, child photos, financial records, or other sensitive data unless they understand the provider and privacy risks.

AI outputs may be incomplete, biased, emotionally unhelpful, or wrong. Users remain responsible for verifying facts, considering consequences, and making final decisions.
